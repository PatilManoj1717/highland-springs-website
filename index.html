Owners Registration

Flat owners must register first to use MyGate. Follow these steps:

  • Download and install the MyGate app.
  • Upload ownership documents:
    • Electricity bill
    • Possession letter
  • Complete the registration process in the app.

Tenants Registration

Tenants can register after the flat owner has completed their registration.

  • Ensure the flat owner has registered on MyGate.
  • Upload a valid rent agreement in the MyGate app.
  • Mark the flat as let out in the app. Learn how: Change Flat Type

Verification Notes

  • All registration requests are approved only after document verification.
  • This process ensures security and prevents unauthorized access to the community.
