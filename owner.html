“Owner” Registration

Steps to Register on MyGate

Step-by-Step Guide for “Owners”

  1. Download the MyGate app
  2. Open the app → Follow steps in app
  3. Select → "Flat Owner" option (for Owners)
  4. Upload document ('Sales Deed') - (Any one from following)
    1. Electricity Bill - Highland Spring
    2. Possession Letter
    3. Other valid Highland Springs ownership document
  5. Choose Occupancy Status
    1. Currently residing
    2. Moving In – You must choose this option if you’re shifting into the flat for the first time. Otherwise, the security guard will not allow the moving vehicle carrying household items inside.
    3. Flat is let out
    4. Flat is empty
  6. Submit Application & wait for approval

Verification Process

Your request will be reviewed and approved after document verification.
